THE NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME(NSS)

"The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a public service program launched by the Government of India to engage youth in community service and nation-building activities. Founded in 1969, NSS operates under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports and is open to students at colleges and universities across India."


Mission and Vision:
NSS aims to foster a spirit of voluntary service and social responsibility among young people. It encourages students to actively participate in various community projects, thereby promoting the development of leadership skills, empathy, and a strong commitment to social justice. The motto of NSS, "Not Me, But You," reflects its focus on selfless service and the importance of giving back to society.


Skill Development and Recognition:
Through their participation in NSS, students develop crucial life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving. They also gain practical experience in project management, communication, and community engagement. NSS volunteers are often recognized for their contributions through awards and certificates, which add value to their academic and professional profiles.
