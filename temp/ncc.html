THE NATIONAL CADET CORPS(NCC)

"The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a prestigious youth development organization that aims to nurture leadership, discipline, and a sense of duty among students.
Open to both male and female cadets, the NCC provides training in military skills, physical fitness, and civic responsibilities, helping to build character and instill a spirit of service to the nation. "


Training and Activities:
NCC cadets undergo rigorous training that includes drills, weapon handling, map reading, and physical fitness exercises. The program emphasizes not only military skills but also the development of a strong moral compass, teamwork, and leadership qualities. Adventure training such as trekking, mountaineering, and para-sailing instills resilience and courage, while community service activities like blood donation camps, Swachh Bharat initiatives, and disaster relief efforts foster a sense of social responsibility.


Opportunities and Exposure:
NCC cadets have the opportunity to participate in various national and international camps, including the Republic Day Camp, Youth Exchange Programs, and the National Integration Camp. These experiences offer cadets a platform to interact with peers from different regions and countries, broadening their horizons and enhancing their understanding of global issues.
